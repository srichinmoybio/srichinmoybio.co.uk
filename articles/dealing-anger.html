Dealing With Anger

Advice for Dealing with Anger.

When we allow anger to enter into our mind we immediately lose any peace of mind and become frustrated. Anger poisons our system; over time anger can tell on both our mental and physical well being. We should always seek to prevent anger from entering our mind. Instead we should seek to affirm thoughts of calmness and peace.
