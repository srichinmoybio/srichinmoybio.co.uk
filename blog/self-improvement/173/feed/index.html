]]> By: Chris Cade http://www.srichinmoybio.co.uk/blog/self-improvement/173/comment-page-1/#comment-511 Fri, 28 Mar 2008 18:14:44 +0000 http://www.srichinmoybio.co.uk/blog/life/173/#comment-511 Another great 30-day idea is to make a small but notable diet change. I remember the day when I started drinking Sprite instead of Dr. Pepper.

Then eventually I swapped out Sprite and started drinking more milk, fruit juice, and water. Eventually I switched to organic milk and fruit juice… I still haven’t found organic water.

Or it can start even simpler. For those who don’t have fruit as part of their diet, just eat one piece of fruit per day. Or perhaps one carrot or piece of celery.

Long story short: I think that making small dietary changes really fits well in the 30-day program because while it can be intimidating to say “I’m changing my diet” or “I’m going on a diet,” it’s much easier to say, “I’m going to enjoy something new for the next 30 days.”
