A lot of people question whether or not I can be up on “current events” if I don’t read the news online, no paper, and don’t watch it.

I just reply:

“If it’s important enough for me to know about, then I’ll find out somehow.”

People could learn a lot by just watching Finding Nemo when Ellen Degeneres’ character says:

“Just keep swimming. Just keep swimming.”
