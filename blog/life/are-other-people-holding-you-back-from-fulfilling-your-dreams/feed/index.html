]]> By: Chris Cade | Spiritual Stories & Parables http://www.srichinmoybio.co.uk/blog/life/are-other-people-holding-you-back-from-fulfilling-your-dreams/comment-page-1/#comment-700 Mon, 16 Jun 2008 16:17:21 +0000 http://www.srichinmoybio.co.uk/blog/?p=230#comment-700 I feel the last line of this is the most powerful… I’d even separate it into a new paragraph if I were writing this post:

“And remember, at the end of the day the only voice you are in any way beholden to is the voice of your own soul.”

I have learned over time, and in some ways am still learning, that there is nobody else who holds me back. The only person who holds me back is me.

Any beliefs I have that others hold me back was only my own insecurities, my own mind, trying to disempower me from going outside of my comfort zone.

Many of us have heard the saying, “Nobody can make you do something that you don’t want to do.” The same is true in this case. Nobody can hold us back unless some part of us wants to be held back…

So my suggestion is that anytime somebody believes that he/she is being held back, that person should really spend times asking themselves what the real reason is that they don’t move forward.
