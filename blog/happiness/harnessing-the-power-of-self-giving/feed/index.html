]]> By: Chris Cade http://www.srichinmoybio.co.uk/blog/happiness/harnessing-the-power-of-self-giving/comment-page-1/#comment-485 Fri, 21 Mar 2008 16:39:00 +0000 http://www.srichinmoybio.co.uk/blog/life/harnessing-the-power-of-self-giving/#comment-485 As a related tangent to this, I once heard Neale Donald Walsch say, “You cannot give that which you do not already have.”

This was powerful because what he proposed was that if there was something you wanted in life. Let’s say in this case, you wanted to experience happiness.

Then the best way to test whether or not you could ever be happy was to try and bring happiness to somebody else.

By bringing it, we realize we had it to begin with. Otherwise it would be impossible to give.

How can we give $100 to somebody if we don’t actually have $100? We can’t. It’s impossible. Same is true of immaterial things.

The gift of giving is always to ourselves first whether we are aware of this or not, and whether or not it comes from the ego or from that deeper place you speak of within the chest.
