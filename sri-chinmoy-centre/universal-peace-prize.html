The Universal Peace Prize

by: Elzbieta

My Supreme on Earth
My Beloved Guru, Sri Chinmoy
Has become the Universal Peace Prize from Supreme -
The Eternal Peace Prize.

He has won, won the Universal Peace Prize.
Then peace begins in Heart
Then peace brings Oneness-Love.

Ma Supreme on Earth
My Beloved Guru, Sri Chinmoy
Has become the Universal Peace Prize.
He has won, won against hostile Forces.
He has won against the see of ignorance,
He has risen to the Light.
His Weapon was purity, His weapon was humility
His weapon was harmony.

He offered Love, He offered Light
He offered Peace.
The world is unaware
How much needs His Love-Light and Peace-Light -
The Eternal, The Divine Peace-Light.

My Supreme on Earth
My Beloved Guru, Sri Chinmoy
Has become the Universal Peace Prize from Supreme -
The Eternal Peace Prize.

My Gratitude Song

Oh, what have You given me?
Oh, that’s what I even didn’t dream.
You’ve created my new dream
And care for its fulfilment.

No sorrow, no sorrow any more.
All flows at Your given speed.
I can’t be tired therefore -
The inner fire keeps me in thrill.

All that I do, is only for You -
Not for name, and not for fame.
What I supposed - becomes truth -
I’m only Your Instrument.

In Your Highest Academy of life
You unite the inner and outer one.
You try to reveal Soul’s-Light
And its mission on earth.

With poetry, music and inner smile
You help me to reach the Highest.
My earth-heavy pilgrim-way
You transform into God’s Height.

That’s Your Love Play

Oh, how much faith You have for me
You haven’t give up.
You always care for me
You’ve made yourself the slave
To make me free -
That’s Your Love, Your Play.

You’ve brought in me
All Good-Qualities to the fore
And enlarged them with the Light.
Why do you do all this, why?

Because we are like one three -
I’m your hand, your ears, your voice.
Thy poetic Soul
Thymes divine thoughts
Thy Heart plays music in me.

You are me Life, my World,
My Dream, my Hope.
You appear me in plenty form
Each day brings a new suprise.

Comment from: Tributes to Sri Chinmoy 1931-2007
